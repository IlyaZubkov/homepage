Jeffrey Preston Bezos

American entrepreneur who played a key role in the growth of e-commerce as the founder and chief executive officer of Amazon.com, Inc., an online merchant of books and later of a wide variety of products. Under my guidance, Amazon became the largest retailer on the World Wide Web and the model for Internet sales.

Biography

  • January 12, 1964

    Was born in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

  • Early career

    • 1986

      Graduated college.

    • 1988 to 1990

      Product manager at Bankers Trust.

    • 1990 to 1994

      Joined D. E. Shaw & Co , a newly founded hedge fund with a strong emphasis on mathematical modelling. Became D. E. Shaw's fourth senior vice-president at age 30.

  • Amazon

    • July 5, 1994

      Left my job at D. E. Shaw and founded Amazon in my garage.

    • 1998

      Diversified into the online sale of music and video, and by the end of the year he had expanded the company's products to include a variety of other consumer goods..

    • 2000

      Borrowed $2 billion from banks, as its cash balances dipped to only $350 million.

    • 2002

      Led Amazon to launch Amazon Web Services, which compiled data from weather channels and website traffic.

    • In late 2002

      Rapid spending from Amazon caused it financial distress when revenues stagnated.

    • 2003

      Amazon rebounded from financial instability and turned a profit of $400 million.

    • 2013

      Secured a $600 million contract with the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA)on behalf of Amazon Web Services.

    • In May 2016

      Sold slightly more than one million shares of my holdings in the company for $671 million, the largest sum I ever raised from selling some of his Amazon stock.

    • On August 4, 2016

      Sold another million of my shares for $756.7 million.

    • January 19, 2018

      My Amazon stock holdings had appreciated to slightly over $109 billion; months later I began to sell stock to raise cash for other enterprises, in particular, Blue Origin.

    • March 6, 2018

      Was formally designated the wealthiest person in the world by Forbes with a net worth of $112 billion.

  • Personal life
